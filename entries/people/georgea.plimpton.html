--- title: George A. Plimpton primaryrole: tr secondrole: era: secondera: p1: George A. Plimpton joined Barnard as an original trustee in 1889 and left in 1936 after serving for 47 years. He was a publisher who received his BA from Amherst in 1876 and later attended Harvard Law School. He married Francis Taylor Pearsons from 1892 until 1900 (her death), and then Fanny Hastings from 1917 until his death in 1936. He had three children. He was a member of the Century Association, the Amherst College Association, the 19th Century Association, the New England Society, and the Bookbuilders Club, among others. p2: p3: source: Robert McCaughey source2: Barnard Archives source3: hyperlinksource: hyperlinksource2: hyperlinksource3: imagessource: imagessource2: imagessource3: relateddocuments: relateddocuments2: relateddocuments3: layout: entry ---
