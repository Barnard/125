--- title: Millicent Cary McIntosh permalink: /entries/people/millicentcarymcintosh.html primaryrole: ad secondrole: era: po secondera: lastname: McIntosh firstname: Millicent Cary department: Dean, President came: 1947 left: 1962 tenure: departure: gender: ab: abtype: phd: phdtype: points: p1: Millicent Cary McIntosh was the fourth dean and first president of Barnard. She served from 1947 to 1962, and ended her tenure with voluntary retirement. She was born in 1899 in Baltimore Maryland, and died in 2002. She received her AB from Bryn Mawr in 1920, and a PhD from Johns Hopkins in 1924. Before coming to Barnard, she served on the Bryn Mawr faculty from 1925 to 1930 and was the Headmistress of the Brearley School in New York City. She married Ruston McIntosh in 1933, he was a pediatrician and professor. She had 5 children. p2: p3: source: Robert A.
