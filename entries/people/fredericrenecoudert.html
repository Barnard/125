--- title: Frederic Rene Coudert primaryrole: tr secondrole: era: ea secondera: p1: Frederic Rene Coudert was an original trustee of Barnard and served from 1889 to 1902. He was a lawyer and received his AB from Columbia in 1850. He was president of the Manhattan Club and a member of the Century Association, the Metropolitan Club, the 19th Century Club, and the American Geographical Society, among others.
