--- title: Silas Brown Brownell primaryrole: tr secondrole: era: ea secondera: p1: Silas Brown Brownell was an original trustee of Barnard and served from 1889 until his death in 1918. He was a lawyer at his own firm, Brownell & Patterson. He was also a member of the American Geographical Society, the Knickerbocker Athletic Club, the New England Society, Phi Beta Kappa and the Century Association, among others. He graduated from Union in 1852. He married Sarah Sheffield and had five daughters.
