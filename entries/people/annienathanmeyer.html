--- title: Annie Nathan Meyer primaryrole: tr secondrole: era: ea secondera: p1: Annie Nathan Meyer was a founder of Barnard College and one of its original trustees. Committed to higher education for women, Meyer petitioned Columbia University to create an affiliated institution exclusively for young women. She succeeded and subsequently served as a trustee of this newly created college from 1889 to 1951. After 62 years as a trustee, Meyer died in 1951. She married Alfred Meyer, a doctor, in 1887, and her sister was suffragist Maud Nathan. She worked as a writer and had a daughter, Margaret Meyer, who died in 1923.
