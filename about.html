About

Learn more about the creation of Barnard History.

This site is a resource for learning more about the history of Barnard College, based upon the work of Professor Robert A. McCaughey. A diverse, multidisciplinary team of students from Barnard College and Columbia University have worked hard to help realize his vision. They include:
